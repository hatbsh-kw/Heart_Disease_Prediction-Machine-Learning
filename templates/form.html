Heart Disease Prediction Form

Feature Descriptions:
I. Age: The patient's age.
II. Sex: 0 = Female, 1 = Male.
III. Chest Pain Type 0-4: Different types of pain.o 1 = Typical angina , 2 = Atypical angina , 3 = Non-anginal pain , 4 = Asymptomatic
IV. Resting Blood Pressure: Blood pressure in mm Hg .
V. Serum Cholesterol: Cholesterol levels in mg/dl.
VI. Fasting Blood Sugar: 0 if <120mg/dl, 1 if >120mg/dl.
VII. Resting ECG: 0-2, measures heart's electrical activity. ,0 = Normal, 1 = ST-T wave abnormality, 2 = Left ventricular hypertrophy
VIII. Max Heart Rate: Maximum HR achieved.
IX. Exercise Induced Angina: 0 = No, 1 = Yes.
X. Oldpeak: ST depression induced by exercise.
XI. Slope: 0-3, ST segment slope. 1 = Upsloping ,2 = Flat ,3 = Downsloping
XII. Major Vessels: 0-3, Number of major vessels colored.detected by fluoroscopy
XIII. Thalassemia(3|6|7): Type of blood disorder.3 = Normal,6 = Fixed defect,7 = Reversible defect
